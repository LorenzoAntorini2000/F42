How it works

Three steps to fitness as a team

🔗
Step 01

Connect as a couple

Create profiles and link accounts with a shared invite code. Completely private — just the two of you.

📋
Step 02

Log meals & workouts

Quick-log food and sessions throughout the day. Your partner sees updates in real time on the shared feed.

🎉
Step 03

Motivate each other

React to entries, cheer on streaks, and watch your combined couple score grow every single week.
